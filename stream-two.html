Joanne Downey Joanne Downey's Qualifications B.A. Hons 'Drama and Theatre Studies' from Trinity College, Dublin. Performed/showcased work in the Samuel Beckett Theatre. Specialised in Directing and Acting Joanne Downey's Experience Joanne worked as the Head of Department at a Performing Arts Camp in Upstate New York, where she devised a programme for the campers that operated in tandem with the main camp (wherein six major musicals and plays were shown). Her curriculum was devised incorporating stand-comedy, improvisation, sketch comedy writing and drama (monologue/duologue performance)
