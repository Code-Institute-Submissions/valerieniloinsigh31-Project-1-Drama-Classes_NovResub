Pure Drama

Innovative new class offerings for tweens (9-12year olds) and teens (13-16year olds) in the scenic rural surroundings of Clonakilty, West Cork. Open to locals and blow-ins alike. Class frequency, times and class sizes can be arranged at discretion-please see the sign-up page. Offered to girls and boys alike, there will be something for everybody.

The classes are offered by the town's own Drama graduate and theatre practitioner, Joanne Downey, who recently graduated with honors from Trinity College, Dublin with a B.A. Hons in 'Drama and Theatre Studies'. Please see more information on Joanne on the mission page. Joanne's history.

The classes, which will be on offer both individually and in groups. Whether you're a parent of a tween or teen or a tween or tween yourself who has some spare time or a passion for drama, with a desire to perform or simply to grow in confidence, there is truly something for everybody on offer with 'Pure Drama.'

Classes

  1. Drama

    Meaning to cause a scene-Joanne will draw from her extensive experience onstage to cate for those that wish to perfect their auditioning skills or have a great monologue or duologue in mind for performance or fun.

  2. Improvisation

    Combining long form and short form improv alike. Drawing inspiration from the Upright Citizens Brigade. Providing a set structure and comfortable atmosphere that will help creativity to blossom.

  3. Sketch-writing

    Ever watched Little Britain, Harry Enfield or Monty Python and ponder how they do it. Come find out...link to clip

  4. Stand-Up Comedy

    You could be the next Jerry Seinfeld or Ellen Degeneres. Joanne specialised in this subject at University, writing her dissertation on stand-up comedy.She will draw from her experience of teaching comedy in Upstate New york for many years. link to clip

  5. Being an emcee

    Be the next Jimmy Kimmel or James Cordon.
