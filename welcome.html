Pure Drama

Innovative new class offerings for tweens (9-12 year olds) and teens (13-16 year olds) in the scenic rural surroundings of Clonakilty, West Cork. Open to locals and blow-ins alike. Class frequency, times and class sizes can be arranged at discretion-please see the Sign-Up page. Offered to girls and boys alike, there will be something for everybody.

The classes are offered by the town's own Drama graduate and theatre practitioner, Joanne Downey, who recently graduated with honors from Trinity College, Dublin with a B.A. Hons in 'Drama and Theatre Studies'. Please see more information on Joanne on the Our Mission page.

The classes will be on offer both individually and in groups. Whether you're a parent of a tween or teen or a teen or tween yourself who has some spare time or a passion for performance, there is truly something for everybody on offer with 'Pure Drama.'
